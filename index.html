Tree-ID

TreeID is an automatic leaf image recognition system for plant species identification. The aim of the project is to classify all of the 60,000 trees on Stanford’s campus. The system is based on a Growing Convolutional Neural Network model, in which the network grows up itself until it solves the target problem thereby, achieving the best trade-off between classification accuracy and computation cost.

Predicting Thoughts

Imagined speech refers to the process in which a subject imagines speaking a given word without moving any muscle. We classifed the syllables “ba”, “ku”,“im” and “si” from imagined speech EEG signals. Our neural networks model is successfully able to classify syllable pairs from the EEG data with over 90 percent accuracy.Learn more about the research here .

Anti Molestation Device

In high school, I invented and subsequently patented an anti molestation device for women as an answer to increasing rape cases in India. Anti Molestation Device is an AI personal protection device in the form of a watch which works on the principle of nerve conduction and provides a shock of 0.08 A to any molestor trying to harass a girl.

I was awarded by the Indian President Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam for this invention and nominated among India’s 20 Most Brilliant Minds by Times Of India. I was also featured by CNN, BBC and over 50 media sources across the world for the device

How it works

Step 1

The device which is like a watch measures the speed of the nerve impulse of the person. Studies have shown that a nerve speed of more than 119m/s is observed only in the case of a "life threatening emergency".

Step 2

When the nerve speed crosses 119m/s, it seeks an external authorization from the user upon which the device gives a shock of 8 milliAmperes ( 0.08 A) temporarily paralyzing the molester.

Step 3

An inbuilt camera on the device is activated which clicks 100 photos non stop. All these photos undergo facial recognition scans and matches are sent to the nearest police station automatically. The device also sends S0S messages to 4 pre-selected recepients with attached GPS location to get help for the victim.
